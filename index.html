Fitness FAQs

Why is fitness important?
Fitness is important for maintaining a healthy lifestyle, reducing the risk of illnesses, enhancing mood, boosting energy and improving sleep quality.
Is it necessary to warm up before exercising?
Yes, warming up before exercising is essential to prepare the body for physical activity, increase blood flow to the muscles, and reduce the risk of injury.
How important is nutrition in fitness?
Nutrition is vital in fitness, as it provides the necessary fuel for energy, promotes muscle recovery and growth, and maintains overall health.
